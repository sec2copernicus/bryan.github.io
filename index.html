Pantai Magrove

1.Mangrove forests have a great function for human life. Quoting from dlhk.bantenprov.go.id, some of the functions of mangrove forests are as follows: They can withstand seawater currents which can erode the coastal plains. Carbon dioxide gas absorber and oxygen generator. A place for marine life such as small fish to take shelter and find food.
2.To make various food products from mangrove fruit, then the fruit Mangroves are first made into flour. Mangrove fruit must be sorted to get good quality and level of maturity desired. Removal of HCN compounds is carried out by conducting soaking with charcoal for 1-7 days. After HCN lost process followed by boiling, drying and flouring. Fruit flour The resulting mangroves can be made into various cookies, dry noodles or crackers. Various forms of crackers can also be produced from raw materials fishery
